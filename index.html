About Me

I'm a Software Engineer in Atlanta, GA with a background in Scrum/Project Management and IT. After years of managing others and being a certified scrum master, I realized I was more passionate about learning to code, training others, and helping people with their software needs. I recently completed a 3 month immersive software enginering course at General Assembly where I learned and implemented best practices for full-stack web development. Throughout the course, I worked with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, MongoDB, Mongoose, Node.js, Python, and Django utilizing Github, Visual Studio Code, Netlify, and Heroku.
